What Types of Bread?

White

  • Most commonly consumed bread worldwide
  • Mild flavor and light color
  • Lacks nutritional value

Wholewheat

  • Made from flour that uses the entire grain
  • Contains more vitamins, minerals, and fiber than whit bread
  • Richer tasting than white bread
  • Dense and chewy texture

Sourdough

  • Created from a long fermentation process
  • Uses natural yeast
  • Contains less gluten than other breads
  • Crisp exterior with fluffy interior

Challah

  • Traditonal Jewish bread eaten during speacial occasions
  • Dough is braided with three strands
  • has a shiny crust with airy and light consistancy

Brioche

  • Incorporates high butter and egg content
  • Has a tender consistancy that's rich with flavor
  • Versatile and used in savory and sweet dishes

How do I store my homemade bread?
First make sure your bread is completely cooled. Then wrap with platic wrap and put in a freezer bag. Leave in freezer for up to three months or out on the counter for three days.
